Understanding the mechanics of Rootelo.

How does this system work?

To ensure fairness in a 4-player format, this leaderboard uses a multi-player Elo model.

Unlike a simple win-count, it calculates your score based on the "Strength of Schedule". If you beat high-ranked players, you gain more. If you lose as the favorite, you drop more.

The Tier System

After your 10-game calibration phase, you officially join the ranks and unlock your tier icon:

Bird (1500+) – The Grandmasters.
Fox (1400+) – The top predators.
Rabbit (1300+) – High-level competitors.
Mouse (1200+) – Established consistent players.

Dynamic K-Factor

Your score's volatility changes as you play more games to ensure stability:

Calibration (0-10 games) K = 80 Find your rank quickly.
Active (11-50 games) K = 40 Competitive weighting.
Veteran (50+ games) K = 20 Long-term stability.

Every player starts the season with a base score of 1200.
