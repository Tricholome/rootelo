An Alternative Ladder

This platform offers a distinct competitive lens through which to view player performance, providing an alternative ranking experience specifically designed for the Root Digital League. Known for its informal yet highly competitive spirit, the league organizes established seasons where every match is played to the bitterest end. You can join the adventure at any time: visit their official site to subscribe, follow the community rules, and start competing for seasonal awards.

The Scoring Engine

To ensure fairness in a four-player format, this leaderboard uses a multi-player Elo model. Unlike a simple win-count, it calculates your rating based on your “strength of schedule”. If you defeat high-ranked opponents, your rating climbs significantly. If you lose as the favorite, your score will reflect the upset.

The Dynamic Phases

The K-Factor determines the maximum number of Elo points you can win or lose in a single match. As you play more games, your K-Factor decreases, protecting your hard-earned rank from wild swings and rewarding long-term consistency.

Calibration (0-10 games) K = 80 Very high volatility to find your true rank quickly.
Active (11-50 games) K = 40 Standard competitive weighting.
Veteran (50+ games) K = 20 Some stability to protect your long-term standing.

The Tier System

After your 10-game calibration phase, you officially join the ranks and unlock your Tier:

Bird (1500+) — The Grandmasters

The absolute peak. There’s rarely more than one at a time, and staying here is a constant battle against gravity. It’s a fleeting, prestigious throne reserved for those who can maintain perfection under the highest pressure.

Fox (1400+) — The Cunning Tacticians

The ones everyone is chasing. They’ve got a trick for every turn and a plan for every disaster. They play with a mischievous grin, consistently outmaneuvering the field to prove they belong among the elite of the Woodland.

Rabbit (1300+) — The Agile Contenders

The true engine of the higher rankings. Their climb is built on speed and adaptability, moving well past the basics to dictate the pace of the game. They are the ones driving the competition forward, always looking for the next opening to move up.

Mouse (1200+) — The Steady Foragers

The foundation of the leaderboard. Securing a spot here is a genuine milestone, proving a level of skill that has already outpaced the standard player. These are the consistent and resilient competitors who provide the first true test for anyone looking to climb the ranks.

The Seasonal Lifecycle

Every player in the Woodland starts from the same root: a 1200 base score. Your progress is permanent, meaning your Elo score is not reset between seasons. Instead, we begin each new season with the calibration phase. For 10 games, everyone’s volatility is set to the same level, giving every player a fair chance to shake up the rankings and climb quickly. You need to complete these games to refresh your standing and reclaim your Tier.

A Living Project

This system is a living project. Formulas and features may evolve to better reflect the competitive reality of the game. If you have any questions, suggestions, or want to discuss the math behind the ranks, join the conversation on the Woodland Warriors.
