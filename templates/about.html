The Alternative Ladder

This platform is an alternative ranking experience specifically designed for the Root Digital League (RDL). It offers a distinct competitive lens through which to view player performance. For official standings, tournament rules, and league updates, please visit the Official RDL Website.

The Scoring Engine

To ensure fairness in a 4-player format, this leaderboard uses a multi-player Elo model. Unlike a simple win-count, it calculates your rating based on your "Strength of Schedule". If you defeat high-ranked opponents, your rating climbs significantly. If you lose as the favorite, your score will reflect the upset.

The Tier System

After your 10-game calibration phase, you officially join the ranks and unlock your tier icon:

Bird (1500+) — The Grandmasters

The absolute peak. There’s rarely more than one at a time, and staying here is a constant battle against gravity. It’s a fleeting, prestigious throne reserved for those who can maintain perfection under the highest pressure.

Fox (1400+) — The Cunning Tacticians

The ones everyone is chasing. They’ve got a trick for every turn and a plan for every disaster. They play with a mischievous grin, consistently outmaneuvering the field to prove they belong among the elite of the Woodland.

Rabbit (1300+) — The Agile Contenders

The true engine of the higher rankings. Their climb is built on speed and adaptability, moving well past the basics to dictate the pace of the game. They are the ones driving the competition forward, always looking for the next opening to move up.

Mouse (1200+) — The Steady Foragers

The foundation of the leaderboard. Securing a spot here is a genuine milestone, proving a level of skill that has already outpaced the standard player. These are the consistent and resilient competitors who provide the first true test for anyone looking to climb the ranks.

The Dynamic Phases

The K-Factor determines the maximum number of Elo points you can win or lose in a single match. A higher K-Factor allows the system to quickly figure out your true skill level. As you play more games, your K-Factor decreases, protecting your hard-earned rank from wild swings and rewarding long-term consistency.

Calibration (0-10 games) K = 80 Find your rank quickly.
Active (11-50 games) K = 40 Competitive weighting.
Veteran (50+ games) K = 20 Long-term stability.

The Seasonal Lifecycle

At the start of every new season, the Woodland resets its focus but remembers its veterans. New players begin their journey with a base score of 1200.

Returning players retain their exact Elo score from the previous season. However, to keep the ladder fresh and accurate, everyone starts back in the Calibration phase (K=80). You must complete 10 placement games to reveal your rank and reclaim your Tier Icon.

A Living Project

This system is a living project and remains under active development. The formulas and features may evolve to better reflect the competitive reality of the game. Your feedback is essential! Join the conversation on the Discord Thread.
